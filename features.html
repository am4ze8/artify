About Artify

Key features implemented in this student project.

Gallery (Upload & Preview)

Whether you paint, produce beats, edit videos or design album art — upload your work, join the community and help shape Artify. We're student-built and growing: your feedback and contributions make the platform better for everyone.

Client-side Search

Live search by title, author or description — runs completely in the browser using the saved posts.

Contact Form (Demo)

This project focuses on modern front-end patterns: accessible markup, local persistence, and polished UI — ideal for prototyping and academic demonstration.
Responsive Design

Artify is a creative community for visual artists, musicians, and editors — a friendly place to share work, find collaborators, learn new skills and get discovered.

Frontend-only

>Upload images, art pieces or audio tracks. Files are previewed in the browser so creators can check quality before sharing. This front-end demo stores uploads in localStorage for easy testing during development.
